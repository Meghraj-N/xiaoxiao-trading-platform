How should we integrate the AI Engine into the Dashboard?

There are 3 main AI flows: Chat/Analysis, Strategy Generation, and Strategy Improvement.

A

AI Copilot Sidebar (Global)

A sliding panel on the right side available from anywhere. Allows you to ask for market analysis or chat with the AI at any time.

B

Dedicated "AI Studio" Tab

A completely separate full-screen tab just for AI. This is where you would view market analysis and generate new strategies.

C

Contextual AI Buttons

Inject AI deeply into existing tabs: "Analyze" button next to charts, "Improve Code" button in strategy builder.
