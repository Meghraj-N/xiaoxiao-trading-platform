AI Integration Design: The "Everywhere" Approach

Combining A, B, and C into a cohesive AI experience.

1. AI Copilot Sidebar (Global)

A floating chat button (bottom right) that opens an AI Copilot sidebar over any tab. You can ask general questions or get quick market analysis without losing context.

2. Dedicated "AI Studio" Tab

A new top-level tab in the navigation. Focuses on deep AI interactions: generating complex strategies from scratch and reviewing full market regime analyses.

3. Contextual Buttons

  • Charts: "Ask AI" to analyze current candlestick patterns.
  • Strategy Builder: "AI Improve" to automatically refactor your strategy code.
  • Backtester: "Explain Trade" to analyze win/loss outcomes.
